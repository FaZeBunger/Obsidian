#Psychology/Unit1 

## Five Steps

1. Observing some phenomenon
2. Formulating hypotheses and predictions
3. Testing through empirical research
4. Drawing conclusions

**Empirical Research** - Observable and measurable. 
**Non-Empirical Research** - Based on logic, theory, or belief alone. 
## Operational Definitions

- Social science research is unique because it requires a strict operational definition of all variables. 
- **Operational Definition:** The translation of a hypothesis into specific, testable, procedures.
- **Hypothesis:** Violent video games cause aggression in children.

### Operational Definition Example

- Aggression - Intentionally or unintentionally causing physical or mental harm to another person.
