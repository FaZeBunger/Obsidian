#Psychology/Unit3 

- Signs of aging are not visible to most until middle adulthood (45-65yrs).
	- Wrinkling skin and graying hair
	- Reaction time begins to increase 
	- Senses dull; vision and hearing difficulties.

- Memory issues are common; practice helps!
- General world knowledge, vocabulary, and reasoning skills do not show age-related declines.
- **Environment matters!**
