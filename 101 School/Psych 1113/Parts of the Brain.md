#Psychology/Unit1 
## **Medulla**

- Controls heartbeat, breathing, blood pressure
- Responsible for basic survival reflexes (swallowing, coughing)

## **Pons**

- Sleep and dreaming
- Coordinates movement
- Helps regulate breathing

## **Reticular Formation**

- Arousal and alertness
- Filters incoming stimuli (important for attention)

## Cerebellum

^6d37a4

- Balance and coordination
- Fine motor movement
- Motor learning (e.g., riding a bike)

## Thalamus

- The brain’s sensory relay station
- Directs sensory information (except smell) to the correct cortex area

# Limbic System (Emotion & Memory)

## **Hypothalamus**

- Homeostasis (body temperature, hunger, thirst)
- Controls endocrine system via the pituitary gland
- Linked to reward and motivation

## **Hippocampus**

- Forms new long-term memories
- Spatial memory
- Damage causes difficulty forming new memories

## **Amygdala**

- Fear and aggression
- Emotional processing
- Emotional memory

## Basal Ganglia

- Movement control
- Habit formation
- Reward processing
- Involved in Parkinson’s disease

## Corpus Callosum

- Thick band of neural fibers connecting the two hemispheres
- Allows communication between left and right sides

## Cerebral Cortex (Higher-Level Thinking)

- The outer layer of the brain — responsible for complex thought.

# The Four Lobes

## Frontal Lobe
^7e6ab5

- Decision-making
- Personality
- Planning
- Voluntary movement
- Speech production

- **Important areas:**
	- **Motor Cortex** – controls voluntary movement
	- **Broca’s Area** – speech production

##  Parietal Lobe

- Touch and body position (somatosensation)
- Spatial awareness

- **Important area:**
	- **Somatosensory Cortex** – processes body sensations

##  Occipital Lobe

- Vision

- **Important area:**
	- **Visual Cortex** – processes visual information

##  Temporal Lobe

- Hearing
- Language comprehension
- Memory

- **Important areas:**
	- **Auditory Cortex** – hearing
	- **Wernicke’s Area** – language comprehension

## **Pituitary Gland**

- “Master gland”
- Controls other endocrine glands
- Regulated by hypothalamus

## Left Hemisphere

- Language
- Logic
- Math
- Analytical processing

## Right Hemisphere

- Creativity
- Spatial ability
- Emotional processing
- Face recognition
