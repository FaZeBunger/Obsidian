**Hyperthymesia**: an exceedingly rare medical condition that leads to near perfect autobiographical recall.

**Amnesia**: Memory Loss due to physical damage or problems in the brain.

**Retrograde Amnesia**: The loss of memories prior to a specific traumatic event. such as a blow to the head.

**Anterograde amnesia**: Is the inability to form new memories. Information after the event will no longer be encoded.

# Alzheimer's Disease

This progressive neurodegenerative disorder primarily affects the hippocampus and surrounding brain regions that are crucial for forming new memories
