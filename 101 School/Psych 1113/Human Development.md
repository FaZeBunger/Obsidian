**Teratogens:** environmental agents such as drugs, chemicals, or viruses that produce birth defects.

**3 Crucial Factors:**
- Dose
- Timing
- Genetic Susceptibility

**Examples of Teratogens**: 
- Drugs, alcohol, cigarettes
	- Includes some over-the-counter (pepto, ibuprofen, Aleve, Sudafed)
	- Caffeine (more than 200mg).
- STI's (aids, herpes).
- Emotional Stater
